# Reviewer Attacks

- This may be a hand-engineered physical cue model rather than learned visual understanding.
- The evidence is generated keypoint video and may not transfer to real hardware.
- The object-affordance ablation is important, but contact timing and uncertainty gating are not validated as necessary.
- Prior work on human motion estimation, mirrored demonstrations, and intent-aware robot imitation may absorb much of the novelty.
- The strongest object-affordance baseline is beaten locally, but external video-to-robot baselines are missing.

Response: frame the contribution as a promising local mechanism under explicit confounds, not as final ICLR-main evidence. The next version needs real human-video or recognized simulator validation.
