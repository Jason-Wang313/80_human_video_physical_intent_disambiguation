# Novelty Decision

Chosen thesis: Human Video Physical Intent Disambiguation turns the seed bet into a mechanism: separate human intent from morphology-specific motion artifacts in video demonstrations.

New central mechanism: a factored physical-intent representation combines body normalization, style-invariant evidence, object affordance, contact cues, and physical action geometry before selecting a robot action.

Decision: proceed as `STRONG_REVISE`. The v4 local benchmark supports a promising mechanism, but real human-video or robot evidence is required before any ICLR-main submission.
